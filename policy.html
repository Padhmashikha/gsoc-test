1. Introduction

Effective date: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Tech Flow. This policy describes the personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices available to users of our website and services. It is written to be practical and actionable for developers, product teams, and privacy-conscious users.

2. Information we collect

We collect information necessary to operate and improve our services. Typical categories include:

  • Account & identity data: name, email, organization, and billing details when you register or purchase.
  • Usage & performance data: page visits, feature usage, timestamps, device/browser characteristics, and IP address.
  • Support & communications: messages, diagnostics, and attachments you provide when contacting support.
  • Derived & aggregated data: anonymized analytics and performance metrics used to improve product quality.

3. How we use information

We use data to deliver, secure, and optimize Tech Flow services, including:

  • Operating and maintaining the platform
  • Personalizing and improving product experiences
  • Detecting and preventing abuse, fraud, and security incidents
  • Customer support and billing
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Cookies & tracking

We and our service providers use cookies and similar technologies for essential site functionality, analytics, and optional personalization. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser or available consent controls.

Cookie categories
  • Essential: required for authentication and core features.
  • Analytics: used to measure usage and improve reliability.
  • Advertising & personalization: used for optional advertising features and recommendations (third-party).

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described and to meet legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations. Examples:

  • Account information: retained until account closure and then for a short period for recovery and compliance.
  • Transactional records: retained as required for tax and accounting.
  • Logs & analytics: retained in aggregated or anonymized form where feasible; raw logs for a limited period.

6. Security

We apply industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. These measures are regularly reviewed and updated. While we strive to protect data, no system is impervious to risk.

7. Your rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, as well as to data portability and to withdraw consent. Requests are handled with appropriate verification to protect privacy and security.

To exercise your rights, contact our privacy team using the information below. We strive to respond promptly and in accordance with applicable law.

8. Third-party services

We use vetted third-party vendors for hosting, analytics, payments, and other services. Those vendors may process data on our behalf under contract. Review their privacy practices where relevant to your use of Tech Flow.

9. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational improvements. Material changes will be communicated via the site or direct notice where appropriate; check the effective date above for the current version.
